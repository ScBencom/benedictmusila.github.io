TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
Photo not showing?
1. Check the filename is exactly "profile.jpg" (lowercase)
2. Make sure it's in the assets folder
3. Try refreshing your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
4. Check browser console for errors (F12 > Console)

Photo looks stretched?
- Make sure your photo is roughly square (same width and height)
- The website will automatically crop it to a circle

Photo file too large?
- Compress it using tinypng.com or squoosh.app
- Aim for under 500KB for fast loading
